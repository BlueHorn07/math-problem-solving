<div class="statement" markdown="1">

A **<u>Gaussian Intger</u>** is a complex number $a + bi$, where $a, b \in \mathbb{Z}$.

For Gaussian integer $\alpha = a + bi$, the **<u>norm</u>** $N(\alpha) = a^2 + b^2$.
